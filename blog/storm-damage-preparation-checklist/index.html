Storm Damage Preparation Checklist in Temple, TX

Home / Blog / Storm Damage Preparation Checklist in Temple, TX

Storms can cause devastating water damage to homes and businesses in Temple, TX. Proper preparation before storm season and before individual storm events can substantially reduce the damage your property sustains and the cost of restoration afterward. This checklist from Temple Restoration Co covers everything Temple homeowners should do to prepare.

Seasonal Preparation

Complete these tasks before storm season begins: Inspect your roof for damaged, missing, or worn shingles and flashing. Clean gutters and downspouts thoroughly and ensure proper discharge. Check and seal around all windows, doors, and exterior penetrations. Inspect your sump pump and test the backup power system. Review your insurance coverage including flood and wind damage. Document your property with current photos for insurance records. Trim trees near your home to reduce wind-driven branch damage.

Before a Storm Event

When storms are forecast: Secure loose outdoor items that could become projectiles. Close storm shutters or board windows if warranted. Ensure your sump pump is operational and the battery backup is charged. Move valuables and important documents to upper levels or waterproof containers. Know your evacuation route and have supplies ready. Program Temple Restoration Co's emergency number ((503) 565-2146) into your phone.
